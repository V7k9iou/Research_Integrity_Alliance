Our Story

The Research Integrity Alliance emerged from a growing concern over declining trust in academic and governmental institutions. Our founders recognized the need to rebuild this trust by thoroughly investigating research practices. We believe that by understanding and improving the conditions for research integrity, we can cultivate an environment where Canadian research flourishes. Our goal is to nurture a robust and resilient research landscape, providing the public with confidence in the credibility and value of scientific endeavours across the country.

Mission

  1. Identify and address challenges to research integrity
  2. Promote ethical conduct and best practices in scientific studies
  3. Safeguard and enhance the credibility of the scientific enterprise
  4. Foster an environment where reliable and trustworthy research can thrive

Core Values

We believe that science, broadly defined, is the key to understanding how the world works. This knowledge is fundamental to human progress and improving the lives of all citizens. Our core value is the unwavering commitment to protecting and promoting the integrity of this vital process of discovery and understanding.

Our long-term vision is to ensure the credibility of research conducted in universities and government agencies, fostering an environment where scientific integrity is paramount and public trust in research is well-founded.

What We Do

What We Do

The Research Integrity Alliance is dedicated to nurturing a robust and trustworthy research environment in Canada. We conduct comprehensive studies on research practices, focusing on identifying both challenges and exemplary conduct, while investigating instances of fraud and plagiarism to understand their root causes and impacts. Our work involves developing and promoting best practices that foster integrity in scientific studies, collaborating with institutions to implement supportive policies, and educating researchers and the public about the importance of research integrity. We also recognize and celebrate instances of exceptional research conduct.

How We Do It

At the Research Integrity Alliance, our work is guided by four key principles that ensure the quality, reliability, and impact of our efforts:

1. Open Inquiry

We approach every investigation and study with an open mind, free from preconceived notions or biases. Our research questions are designed to explore issues thoroughly, considering multiple perspectives and allowing the evidence to guide our conclusions. This principle ensures that our findings are objective and comprehensive.

2. Transparency

We believe in full disclosure of our methods, data, and findings. All our research processes, from data collection to analysis, are documented and made available for scrutiny. This transparency not only builds trust in our work but also allows others to verify and build upon our research, fostering a culture of openness in the scientific community.

3. Building on Feedback

We actively seek and incorporate feedback from diverse sources, including researchers, institutions, and the public. This iterative approach allows us to refine our methods, address potential blind spots, and ensure our work remains relevant and impactful. By embracing constructive criticism, we continuously improve the quality and relevance of our research and recommendations.

4. Understanding the World as It Is

Our goal is to capture the reality of research practices, not an idealized version. We strive to present an accurate picture of the current state of research integrity, acknowledging both strengths and weaknesses in the system. This realistic approach enables us to identify practical, effective solutions to enhance research integrity in the real world.

These principles work together to ensure that our research and white papers provide a solid foundation for improving research integrity across Canada and beyond.
