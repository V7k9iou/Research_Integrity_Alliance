What we do

At the Common Humanity Coalition, our work spans a range of initiatives aimed at bringing our principles to life across Canadian society.

Inform the public by doing research, tracking policies and gathering information so Canadians are aware of the extent to which institutions, like workplaces and school boards, are aligned with our guiding principles.

Change policies by mobilizing coalition members and the public at large to advocate for policies and practices that make institutions more reflective of our principles of honouring individuality, fostering understanding, and celebrating our common humanity.

Empower communities by providing resources, training and support to help local chapters implement initiatives that bring our vision to life in their neighbourhoods and cities. This grassroots approach ensures our work is responsive to local community needs.

This multifaceted approach allows us to drive meaningful, lasting change - one person, one community, and one institution at a time. Join us in this vital work to celebrate our common humanity.

How we do it

Lead by example by embodying our principles in the way we operate as an organization. We model the understanding-based culture we wish to see reflected across Canadian society.

Collaborate with like-minded organizations by building partnerships to leverage our collective expertise and networks to amplify our impact nationwide. United, we can drive meaningful, lasting change.

Bring people together by hosting community events, workshops, and dialogues that bring together individuals with different histories, perspectives, goals and ambitions. We create opportunities for people to share their stories, listen to one another, and find common ground. Through these gatherings, we inspire a sense of community and collective purpose.

Our guiding principles

Singular Worth

Always remembering the distinctive qualities that make each one of us irreplaceable.

At the Common Humanity Coalition, we believe that every individual possesses an inherent and singular worth. We are dedicated to celebrating the unique talents, perspectives and life experiences that make each person irreplaceable. By honouring the diversity of human expression, we create an environment where all feel empowered to share their authentic selves and contribute to the richness of our collective journey.

Understanding

Fostering the meeting of hearts and minds, we want to inspire a journey of learning, connection that bridges differences.

Central to our mission is cultivating deeper understanding between all people. We believe that by fostering open dialogue, cultivating empathy and celebrating diverse viewpoints, we can bridge divides and find common ground. Through this process of learning and connection, we inspire a more compassionate society where differences are celebrated rather than feared.

Common Humanity

We dedicate ourselves to bringing our shared experiences to the fore, uniting Canadians through common aspirations, nurturing a deep sense of our collective human journey.

At the heart of the Common Humanity Coalition is a deep reverence for the universal experiences that bind us all together as human beings. Whether it is the joy of love, the pain of loss, or the pursuit of purpose, we are united by the common threads that run through the tapestry of the human experience. By honouring these shared aspirations and emotions, we foster a profound sense of connection that transcends individual differences and inspires us to work collectively towards a better future.

Who we are

The Common Humanity Coalition is a national organization dedicated to uniting Canadians through our shared human experiences. We are a non-profit organization led by a Board of Directors with a range of experiences and perspectives, who provide strategic oversight and guidance.

Our work is carried out by dedicated staff and volunteers across local chapters in communities from coast to coast to coast. These passionate individuals bring our principles to life through research, community events, and collaborative initiatives.

At the heart of our organization are the members - Canadians from all walks of life who have joined our cause. Together, we form a vibrant network committed to fostering greater understanding, celebrating individuality, and strengthening the bonds of our common humanity.

Whether you’re looking to get involved, support our mission, or simply learn more, we welcome you to connect with your nearest chapter or reach out to our national office. Join us as we work to create a more compassionate, connected Canada.
